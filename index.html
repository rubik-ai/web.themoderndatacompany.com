DataOS®

The World’s First
Data Operating System

DataOS is a data operating system that democratizes access to high quality, governed, and secure data in real-time. DataOS connects all data across the enterprise to empower all users to achieve business outcomes over being tied down with data-wrangling.

It creates an intelligent semantic layer that empowers business and technical users to discover, explore, and collaborate to deliver data products quickly and easily. The unmatched composability of DataOS lets customers adapt it to any data architecture, be it a data fabric, data mesh, lakehouse, or something new. DataOS puts your entire enterprise on the fastest path to a data-powered future.
