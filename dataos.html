The
DataOS
Difference

DataOS creates the fastest path from data to decisions by delivering an agile and composable data operating system that delivers quality, governed, and secure data in real-time. DataOS connects all your structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data assets across the enterprise and builds an intelligent semantic layer that lets business and technical users discover, explore, and collaborate to deliver data products in days and weeks instead of months and years.

The unmatched composability of DataOS lets customers adapt it into any data architecture such as a data fabric, data mesh, lake house, or many others, and delivers democratized access to trusted data right when you need it.

DataOS Features

All things data

DataOS lets you manage the entire lifecycle of your data workloads in one place. From ingesting data to metadata management, governance to observability, activating and sharing data, DataOS is your one-stop solution to go from data to decisions.

Composable, Open and Interoperable

DataOS is the first truly composable platform that lets users build the specific data architecture of their requirements. Built on open data formats, DataOS gives users complete control over their data — no more vendor lock-ins.

Enhance, don’t replace your existing architecture

Skip the hassle of having to rip your existing investments or move your data. DataOS weaves a modern connective tissue through your entire data stack. Users can augment and modernize current investments by leveraging the quality data and ontology built by DataOS.

Model-driven Data Management

DataOS enables business-driven use and management of data, helps streamline data pipelines, and democratize access to data with Model Query Language instead of SQL.

DataOS Layers

Understanding DataOS

In order to understand DataOS’ capabilities, it is useful to break them down into three layers.

Data Layer

Connect and explore your data across on-prem, cloud, and various data stores with single-click templates without moving or copying it. Ice base gives you the best of data warehouse and data lake technologies with an acid-compliant lakehouse. Optimize your compute resources for cost and performance with the ability to assign resources by teams and workloads.

The Knowledge Layer

Build an ontology of your data that encodes relationships, metadata, and lineage and adds knowledge to any decision you make. Give your technical and business users a single pane of glass to discover and understand your unified data and insights across your data silos. Ensure confidence in your data by seamlessly enforcing quality and governance rules and policies. Monitor and analyze the health of your data throughout its entire lifecycle and enhance data reliability.

The Activation Layer

Activate and share your data and insights across the enterprise. Create custom reports and dashboards on any dataset in minutes. MML and MQL enable business users to drive their own insights with easily accessible and queryable data. Unlock new models of data revenue by sharing datasets in a secure and auditable environment.
