Download

Today, most companies house their data assets in a mix of on-premise, public, and private cloud systems. This hybrid approach poses major challenges for data integration, governance, and security. A data fabric architecture presents an easier path forward by taking the hard work out of data management.

A data fabric architecture is meant to bring together data management functions such as data governance and security management into a single network that connects the organizations breadth of data sources. This separation of data management from specific technologies and enforcing it through a single, dedicated environment creates a more resilient system where new technologies or data sources can be easily connected with minimal disruption and massively speeds up the process of data collection and analysis.
