Most enterprises aim to be data driven — yours is likely among them. However, even the world's top performing companies face a number of challenges that prevent them from fully exploiting all data, including a lack of data access.

With a data fabric, your business users, data analysts, and data scientists finally have access to clean, trusted data in real time for their business applications and models. This ultimately improves decision making and drives digital transformation. Your core technical teams can leverage a data fabric to rein in and simplify data management and governance across multiple landscapes without worrying about cost and risk.

Check out our resources in this section for an in-depth look into the specifics and advantages of deploying a data fabric within your organization.

A data fabric is a data management architecture that can help enterprises and their technical teams optimize access to different data types across a wide landscape and rein in the chaos for self-service, in real time. With a data fabric, specifically DataOS, leaders can elevate the value of their data by providing every user access to the right data whenever they need it, regardless of where it is stored and its format. DataOS' architecture is agnostic to data environments, processes, use and geography, while deploying core data management capabilities. It can automate data discovery, governance and consumption, delivering business-ready data for analytics and AI in a matter of weeks.
