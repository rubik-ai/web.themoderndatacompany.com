How a Composable Data Fabric Meets the Needs of Today’s Evolving Enterprise

The modern data landscape continues to increase in volume and variety at an alarming rate. The rise of AI and IOT, unstructured data volume, and increasing external data sources has long shown signs of outgrowing traditional data management systems. The end result is enterprises still stuck in a reactive data strategy.

A composable data fabric offers a new way forward. By weaving together data from internal and external data silos, composable data fabrics are a powerful and flexible network of information to power countless apps and analytics for enterprises.
