Case Study #1

Reduce marketing data workloads by 80% while providing marketers with agility to drive value quickly.

The Challenge
  • IT teams were stretched too thin with multiple enterprise-wide initiatives.
  • Marketing teams were struggling to address customer needs in a timely fashion.
Our Solution
  • Provided applications and APIs on top of DataOS to drive site personalization, vendor POC management, customer segmentation, and real-time altering on global metrics with simple to use interfaces.
  • Delivered trusted business data to marketing teams in read-only data views in their own compute.
Key Takeaways
  • Separated compute for business teams and IT.
  • Deployed in 4 weeks.
  • GDPR / CCPA compliant solution. Business teams have access to always-compliant data.
  • “Undo button” for business users to use the tools without worrying about incorrect usage.

Case Study #2

Reduce cart abandonment by 32% with real-time data and customer segmentation.

Our Solution
  • Leveraged DataOS’ streaming pipelines to ingest customer profile (Salesforce), Clickstream (Omniture), orders (homegrown order management system) to create a true omni-channel view of the customer that is updated in real-time.
  • Created a purpose built low latency C360 data store to drive 50ms latencies to retrieve user information and power a third-party recommendation engine.
  • Created audience segments and leveraged respective customer audience labels to drive site personalization.
Key Takeaways
  • Delivered end-to-end solution in 3 weeks.
  • Marketing team members own the C360 and vendor POC management for recommendation engines / personalization.
  • Fully self-serve for business users.
