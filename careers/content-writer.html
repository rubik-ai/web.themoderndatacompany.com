We are looking for a Content writer who’ll work with technical teams (product development and support) to create quality content assets like – brochures, blogs, whitepapers, case studies, video scripts, etc.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Develop an understanding of the product and domain that DataOS operates in to convert technical inputs from product owners into clear and insightful content for internal and external consumption
  • Understand the latest domain trends to ensure our content is contextual, relevant, compelling, and data-driven enough to drive engagement within the target markets
  • Collaborate with Product, Sales, and Operations teams to develop high impact, data-driven content assets

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in English, technical communications or MBA with specialization in Marketing
  • 3-7 years of experience in creating and editing complex technical content
  • Proven work experience in software product company as a Content Writer
  • Tech-savvy and quick to understand new technologies and solutions
  • Collaborate with multiple stakeholders & subject matter expert
  • Experience in creating engaging content for product-related information articles
  • Experience doing research using multiple sources
  • Passion for making complex technical concepts and procedures understandable to the audience
  • Superb written English language skills, proofreading, and editing skills
  • Strong time management and prioritizations skills
  • Familiar with popular SEO practices
