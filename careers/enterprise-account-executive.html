Today, enterprises are spending heavily on a range of data products and stitching them together to get some semblance of a data fabric. These data products require significant integration and customization work, resulting in a final product that lacks flexibility and exposes multiple risk vectors.

We saw a need for a data platform that humanizes access to data, breaks data silos, and transforms companies as they step towards data democratization and gaining business insights in real-time...These are some of the reasons why we founded The Modern Data Company.

The Modern Data Company is hiring a determined, ambitious, and driven Enterprise Account Executive.

The EA is responsible for prospecting and developing enterprise client relationships to solve real data challenges using The Modern Data family of products. Specifically, the EA will actively prospect, assess opportunities, develop account plans, forecast, negotiate contracts, and close business in a timely manner. The EA is then responsible for ensuring the successful transition to the customer success team.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Represent Modern Data's full suite of products and services using technical, organizational, and customer knowledge to influence customers and assist them in applying the products/services to their needs, resulting in revenue generation
  • Opportunity Assessment: Personally oversee and be the primary point-of-contact for named accounts portfolio
  • Account Plan Development: Develop strategic sales plans for portfolio accounts with the largest sales opportunities
  • Forecasting: Accurately assess timelines to purchase
  • Negotiate Contracts: Ensure contracts are aligned with Modern Data's financial objectives while delivering value-added products, maintenance, and services to meet customer requirements
  • Post-sale Management: Maintain awareness and status of all key contractual obligations, facilitating customer meetings and communications regarding deliverables where necessary
  • Consistently demonstrate strong product knowledge and ability to articulate our value proposition
  • Deliver technology solutions tailored to customer needs
  • Execute account plans, ensuring the accurate production of the account plans, business case preparation, appropriate contract presentation, and the effective delivery of account plan activities
  • Meet and beat assigned quota
